Responsive Website Designs

Responsive web design makes websites faster, more accessible, and easier to navigate. It makes it easier for users to then find the information they are looking for and typically encourages them to stay on your site. Plus, fantastic usability may encourage users to come back to your website in the future

Responsive Web design is the approach that suggests that design and development should respond to the user's behavior and environment based on screen size, platform and orientation. The practice consists of a mix of flexible grids and layouts, images and an intelligent use of CSS media queries.

Responsive web design refers to a website designed with mobile-friendly features, content, and media. Responsive websites change and adapt to whatever device a visitor may be using, including smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

  • - Eliminate Friction.
  • - Design for Thumbs.
  • - Take Advantage of Mobile Devices' Native Hardware.
  • - Make Layouts Fluid/Adaptive by Default.
  • - Remember, Typography Can Be Responsive Too.
